#### Description
Are you tired of keeping track of Anytimers in your notes? Do your friends always come up to you with a bunch of Anytimers that you don’t remember them having? And are you sick of defending how you obtained them and when you used them?

Because we were. That’s why Jilles, Stijn, and Martijn made the AnyTimer app. The AnyTimerApp is the social platform for friends to manage Anytimers and capture legendary moments together. The app allows users to add friends, track Anytimers by saving or handing them out, and notifies you when changes have been made.

Beyond tracking, the app features a highly specialized and advanced "Any-Timer" designed to accurately measure and record activities, settling any friendly disputes over who is the fastest.

Users can share their Anytimer update's as a post to a public feed, where friends can interact through likes and comments on posts that can include photos or videos. Whether you are building an archive of memories or just keeping the score honest, the AnyTimerApp brings a fun, organized, and professional edge to your group’s competitive spirit.
